Positives

  • Detail Oriented

Negatives

Black

Black wants power through any means necessary.

Positives

  • Pragmatic
  • Driven
  • Individual Focused

Negatives

Red

Negatives

Positives

  • Content
  • Grounded
  • Wise

Negatives

  • Passive

Magic: The Gathering Arena

Arena is basically a computer version of the card game.

It's one of those freemium games that wants you to spend time or money for cards. Personally I find most of the fun of Magic is playing cards with people face-to-face as a social activity but the tutorial here is a great way to learn to play the game.

Also, unless you want to teach the people you live with to play you don't have a lot of face-to-face options right now so Arena is about the best you're going to get these days.

You can download Arena here.
